# Hospital Management System (HMS) — Requirements Specification
**Tender Reference:** HMS/2026/01
**Issuing Organisation:** Marybegg Kansanshi Mine Hospital

---

## 1. System Overview

A fully integrated, scalable, secure, and interoperable Hospital Management System (HMS) is required to:

- Digitize all hospital operations
- Improve operational efficiency and workflow automation
- Enhance patient care delivery and safety
- Ensure regulatory compliance and audit readiness
- Provide real-time analytics and reporting
- Support multi-departmental coordination

---

## 2. General System Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Concurrent Users | 500+ concurrent staff users |
| Access Control | Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) |
| Audit & Logging | Full audit trails and activity logs |
| Architecture | Scalable and modular |
| Multi-branch | Multi-branch support capability |
| Data Security | Healthcare data security compliance |
| Availability | High availability with automated backups |
| Accessibility | Web-based and mobile accessible |
| Integration | API integration capabilities |
| Deployment | On-premise or cloud-based |

---

## 3. Required Modules

### 3.1 Front Office & Administration
- Patient registration and reception management
- Appointment scheduling
- Administrative workflow support

### 3.2 Clinical Modules
- Electronic Medical Records (EMR)
- Outpatient Department (OPD)
- Inpatient Department (IPD)
- Emergency Department
- Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
- Pharmacy Management
- Laboratory Information System (LIS)
- Radiology Information System (RIS)
- Blood Bank Management

### 3.3 Financial & Billing
- Patient billing and invoicing
- Financial reporting
- Insurance and claims management

### 3.4 Inventory & Supply Chain
- Stock and inventory tracking
- Procurement and supply chain workflows
- Consumption and reorder management

### 3.5 Human Resource Management
- Staff records and HR administration
- Payroll integration support
- Scheduling and attendance

### 3.6 Reporting & Analytics
- Real-time dashboards and operational reporting
- Custom report generation
- Regulatory and compliance reports

---

## 4. Technical Specifications

Vendors must provide full documentation covering:

### 4.1 Technology Stack
- Programming languages, frameworks, and platforms used

### 4.2 Database Architecture
- Database engine(s) and schema design approach

### 4.3 Hosting Requirements
- On-premise hardware specifications or cloud provider/tier requirements

### 4.4 Security Framework
- Encryption standards (data at rest and in transit)
- Authentication and session management
- Access control implementation details

### 4.5 Backup & Disaster Recovery
- Backup frequency, retention policy, and recovery time objectives (RTO/RPO)

### 4.6 Performance Benchmarks
- System response times under expected concurrent load (500+ users)

### 4.7 Integration Standards
- Supported healthcare interoperability standards: HL7, FHIR, or equivalent
- API documentation availability

---

## 5. Training & Support Requirements

- On-site training for administrators and end-users across all departments
- System user manuals and full technical documentation
- Minimum **12 months** warranty and post-go-live support
- 24/7 technical support (preferred)
- Formal Service Level Agreement (SLA) to be provided

---

## 6. Vendor Eligibility Requirements

- Minimum **5 years** of experience in healthcare software implementation
- At least **3 hospital references** with 300+ staff each
- Demonstrated prior HMS deployment experience
- Valid company registration documents
- Audited financial statements for the last 3 years
